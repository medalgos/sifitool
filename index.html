About MedAlgos

Tools you can trust for clinical practice

Our Mission

MedAlgos provides reliable, peer-reviewed, and easy-to-use medical tools for clinical practice. Our mission is to support healthcare providers in delivering evidence-based care through accessible digital tools.

All tools are developed based on current clinical guidelines and regularly updated to reflect the latest medical research.

Development Process

Each calculator undergoes a rigorous development process:

  • Research of current clinical guidelines and literature
  • Algorithm development by medical professionals
  • Peer review by specialists in the field
  • User testing in clinical environments
  • Regular updates based on new evidence
